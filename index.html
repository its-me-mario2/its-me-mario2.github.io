CHAOS CUBED

The Sulfur Cube Eats Blocks and Changes Everything

June 16, 2026

Minecraft's Chaos Cubed update dropped on June 16, 2026, and the headline feature is the Sulfur Cube - a passive mob that eats blocks and transforms its physical behavior based on what it consumes.

  • Feed it wood → becomes buoyant and bouncy
  • Feed it ice → slides around like a hockey puck
  • Feed it TNT → it becomes an explosive hazard
  • Feed it magma → changes heat properties

The update also introduces Sulfur Caves, a new underground biome with yellow sulfur blocks, red cinnabar blocks, dangerous geysers that launch you skyward, and the new "Bounce" music disc by fingerspit.

Seed 67 Naturally Generates "67" in Terrain

June 18, 2026

A player typed 67 as a seed and found a ravine shaped like the number 6 next to a frozen lake shaped like 7. At coordinates X:100, Z:600, this formation is reproducible across both Java and Bedrock Editions. It went instantly viral.

Disc 11: The Most Terrifying 2.5 Minutes in Gaming

Since 2011

Disc 11 isn't music. It's a recording of someone running, breathing heavily, then being cut off by static. C418 confirmed it's meant to evoke "a person running and something happening to them." Mojang has never explained it. Players still drop it into jukeboxes in dark rooms just to feel something.

There Is No Disc 12

Since 2011

Between Disc 11 (terrifying breathing) and Disc 13 (unsettling cave noises), there is no Disc 12. The gap is intentional. The missing disc is arguably the scariest part of Minecraft's hidden lore.

The 43rd Hidden Dimension (April Fools 2020)

April 1, 2020

Mojang's April Fools snapshot let players throw books into Nether portals to access infinite dimensions. 42 were found publicly. The 43rd required decoding a sound file as an SSTV signal to reveal a handwritten phrase. The hidden world's message: "All these worlds are yours except Europa. Use them in peace."

Happy Ghasts Fly Away Forever If Untethered

2025

The Happy Ghast from the Mounts of Mayhem update will fly away indefinitely if you don't tether it. Leave one untethered and come back hours later - it's gone. Into the horizon. Forever.

SECRET HIDDEN EASTER EGGS

the jeb_ Rainbow Sheep

Since 2012

Name a sheep jeb_ with a name tag and it cycles through all 16 wool colors in a rainbow animation. Named after lead developer Jens Bergensten. The catch? It still drops its original wool color when sheared. No rainbow wool exists.

Dinnerbone Flips Any Mob Upside Down

Since 2011

Name any mob Dinnerbone or Grumm and it renders completely upside-down. Behavior doesn't change. Mobs walk on ceilings visually. Even works on the Ender Dragon (if you can get that close with a name tag).

Johnny Vindicators Attack Everything

Since 2016

Name a Vindicator Johnny and it becomes hostile to every mob in the game except other Illagers. A reference to Jack Torrance's iconic axe scene in The Shining: "Here's Johnny!"

Pirate Speak Language

Hidden

Type excitedze in the recipe book search bar to activate Pirate Speak, transforming every in-game text into pirate slang. There's also LOLCAT, Shakespearean English, Anglish, and upside-down English.

Minceraft: 0.01% Chance on Title Screen

Since Alpha

There's a 0.01% chance the title screen reads Minceraft instead of Minecraft. Crash logs can also say "You should try our sister game, Minceraft!" The typo is completely intentional.

The End Poem Knows You're Real

Since 2011

After defeating the Ender Dragon, a 1,500-word poem scrolls for 9 minutes. It's the only part of Minecraft that breaks the fourth wall and acknowledges you as a real human. Written by Irish novelist Julian Gough for €20,000, he later released it to the public domain in 2022.

TECH SECRET MECHANICS

Block Update Suppression

Piston/redstone configs that prevent block state changes. Creates floating sand, sourceless water. Used on technical servers for efficient farms.

Nether Portal Chunk Loading

Nether portals keep chunks loaded across dimensions. Place a hopper minecart under a portal in the Nether for a cross-dimensional chunk loader.

TNT Duplication with Coral

TNT on coral fans underwater duplicates when the coral breaks from lack of water. The original block isn't consumed. Mojang knows. They don't patch it.

Sculk XP Banking

Silk touch a sculk catalyst after it absorbs mob deaths. Move it anywhere. Break without silk touch to release all stored XP on demand.

Enderman Teleport Prevention

Endermen cannot teleport while on non-full blocks (boats, slabs, minecarts). Also won't teleport if you stare at their feet from exactly 40 blocks away.

Sulfur Cube Redstone

Sculk sensors detect Sulfur Cube bounces (frequency 2). Dispensers can equip blocks into cubes via redstone. Full automation of physics minigames is now possible.

Allay Duplication

Allays duplicate when they hear a jukebox playing near an amethyst shard. Rapid Allay farming without any complex redstone setups.

Snow Golem Nether Survival

Give a Snow Golem fire resistance and it survives in deserts AND the Nether. The potion effect completely negates its melting mechanic.
